About and inquiries

Contact

For booking, recording, collaboration, press, or other professional inquiries, this is the place to get in touch.

Ryan Hall is a New Orleans-based guitarist, singer, songwriter, and recording artist whose work moves between blues, rock, ambient music, and experimental sound.

His live background includes extended engagements at WinStar World Casino and House of Blues Las Vegas, along with performances across the world, from Italy and Spain to Russia and Japan. In the studio, he works with guitar, voice, Hammond organ, acoustic instruments, and electronic textures, with an emphasis on recordings that still feel human and performed.

This site brings together his songs, videos, live work, and the occasional stranger transmission from the edges.
